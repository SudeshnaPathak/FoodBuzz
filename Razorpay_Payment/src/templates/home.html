Special Dish

Hawaiian Delight

Hawaiian Delight Pizza, despite its name, originated in Canada in the 1960s and features a savory-sweet combination of ham or Canadian bacon, pineapple chunks, and cheese on a tomato or barbecue sauce base. Its unique flavor profile, blending the sweetness of pineapple with the savory taste of ham, has made it a popular choice among pizza enthusiasts worldwide. While its inclusion of pineapple on pizza has sparked occasional debates, Hawaiian Delight Pizza remains a beloved and widely available option in pizzerias, offering a delicious fusion of flavors to satisfy diverse palates.
